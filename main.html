Rock Paper Scissors

Welcome to the classic game of Rock Paper Scissors! This timeless game has a rich history, dating back to ancient times and enjoyed by people of all ages and cultures.

The rules are simple:

  • Rock crushes Scissors
  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Paper covers Rock
